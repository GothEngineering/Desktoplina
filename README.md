
# Desktoplina
A very polite little pet to accompany me on my day, this critter will annoy even the most patient users.
Personal project done to try out DisplayServer and enums, also because i wanted to make a desktop pet

## Features
1. Tells you the time on the top corner (cozy)
2. Does nothing (YET!!!) besides walking and following your cursor.. and sleeping
3. Ocassionally jumpscares you (very rarely tho)
4. Gives you a brownie recipe for half a second in a very rare chance
